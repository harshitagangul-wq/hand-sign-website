Process

How It Works

Three simple steps — from camera to classification in seconds.

01
📲

Open the App

Launch SignSense on your Android phone. The Teachable Machine model loads automatically.

02
✋

Show Your Hand Sign

Hold up a hand sign (A, B, C, or D) in front of your camera and tap the Classify button.

03
📊

See the Result

The app instantly shows the confidence % for each detected sign — live and in real time!

04
🔁

Repeat & Learn

Practice different signs and improve your accuracy. Great for sign language learning!

Capabilities

Packed with Features

Everything you need for accurate hand sign detection.

🤖

ML-Powered Detection

Powered by Google Teachable Machine — a custom trained image classification model for accurate sign detection.

📷

Live Camera Feed

Uses WebViewer to stream real-time video from your phone camera for instant classification.

📊

Confidence Scores

Displays confidence percentage for each sign (A, B, C, D) so you know exactly how accurate the detection is.

⚡

Real-Time Results

Get classification results in seconds — no waiting, no lag. Just fast and smooth detection.

📱

Android Compatible

Built for Android 5+ devices. Works on any modern Android smartphone without special hardware.

♿

Accessibility Focused

Designed to help with sign language learning and communication for the hearing-impaired community.
